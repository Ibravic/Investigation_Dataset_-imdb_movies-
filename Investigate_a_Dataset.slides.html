Introduction¶

During the project we will use data set of "Tmbd-Movies" dataset that shows movies produced from 1966 to 2015 with their rates and revenues.

During this investigation we will answer the following questions:

  • 1- What is the most factor that affects the revenues?
  • 2- Which genres are most popular?
  • 3- Which year is the highest one in getting total revenues?
  • 4- who is the director produced the largest number of movies?
  • 5- which movie has the highest net profit ? (net profit = revenues - production cost)
  • 6- Finally, is there correlation between number of votes and the vate rate ?! so we cannot trust in the votes with small number of voters ?!

    Conclusions¶

    After answering the 6 propused questions, we can conclude the following:¶

  • There are a moderate dirct correlation between budget and revenues, and between popularity and revenues. So budget is considered the most important factor that affects the revenues.
  • Drama is the most popular gender, followed by Action then Thriller.
  • "Steven Spielberg" is the director who produced the highest number of movies.
  • Also, Steven Spielberg achieves the highest total revenues among all other directors.
  • 2015 is the highest year in gaining total revenues.
  • "Star Wars" gained the highest net profit ever! the movie directed by "George Lucas" and produced in 1977
  • Finally, there are weak direct correlation between number of votes and average vote rate. So we can consider the average vote rate even if voted by small number of voters.
